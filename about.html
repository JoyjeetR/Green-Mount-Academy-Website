Our Mission

At Green Mount Academy, our mission is to create a learning environment that balances academic excellence with moral strength, discipline, and confidence.

We believe education is not only about marks and examinations — it is about building character, leadership qualities, and lifelong values.

Our structured academic framework, combined with co-curricular engagement and value-based guidance, ensures that every child grows intellectually, socially, and emotionally.

We aim to prepare students not just for board examinations, but for real-life challenges — empowering them to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our History

Green Mount Academy was founded in 1996 by the visionary Late Shri Nava Kumar Roy with a mission to bring quality, disciplined and value-based education to Dumka and surrounding regions.

What began as a humble initiative has today grown into a respected educational institution with multiple branches and thousands of students.

The foundation of the school was built on strong principles — discipline, integrity, academic excellence, and moral values. These guiding principles continue to shape every student’s journey.

Over the years, Green Mount Academy has expanded its academic reach while maintaining its core philosophy. Under the guidance of the Santhal Parganas Education Society, the school has continued to innovate in teaching methods, infrastructure, and student development.

Today, the academy stands as a symbol of trust, dedication, and educational leadership in Jharkhand.
