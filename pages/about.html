About Green Mount Academy

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1996

Our History & Foundation

Established in 1996 by the visionary Late Nava Kumar Roy along with eminent educationists of Dumka, Green Mount Academy was founded with a clear mission — to provide accessible, value-based and quality education.

The school functions under the supervision of the Santhal Parganas Education Society, a registered educational body dedicated to academic excellence and character development.

Over the years, the academy has grown into a respected institution with multiple branches, serving thousands of students across Jharkhand.

Message from the Secretary

“Our aim is to strengthen the character and expand the intellect of every student. Together with parents and guardians, we create a supportive environment that empowers students to stand confidently in the world.”

Mr. Karun Kumar Roy, Secretary

Our Mission

To nurture disciplined, confident, and ethically grounded students through quality education, innovative teaching methods, and holistic development.

Our Vision

To inspire students to become responsible global citizens whose knowledge, values, and leadership positively impact society.

Curriculum & Academic Excellence

Green Mount Academy is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. The school provides English-medium instruction from early childhood through senior secondary levels.

Core Subjects

English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Computer Science, and General Knowledge.

Co-Curricular Activities

Sports, cultural programs, debates, science exhibitions, yoga, and personality development.

Holistic Learning

Focus on discipline, moral education, leadership skills, and practical learning experiences.

Senior Secondary Streams
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology)
  • Commerce (Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics)
  • Computer Science

Our Core Values

We cultivate self-control, punctuality, and responsibility. Students learn to respect rules and develop habits that prepare them for lifelong success.

We encourage honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage. Students are taught to stand by truth and fairness.

Respect for teachers, parents, peers, and diversity is central to our school culture.

We strive for continuous improvement in academics, sports, leadership, and personal development.

Our Growing Presence

Green Mount Academy proudly serves students across multiple locations in Dumka and nearby regions, ensuring quality education is accessible to every community.
