our mission

Our mission is to provide quality education that helps every student grow academically, socially, and emotionally. We want our students to develop strong values, good habits, and a positive attitude towards learning. We aim to teach good values, respect, and responsibility while providing a healthy and positive learning environment.

We aim to create a safe, friendly, and supportive environment where every child feels confident and encouraged. Our teachers work with dedication to ensure that all students understand their subjects well and improve day by day. Our teachers guide each student with care and dedication so they can discover their strengths and talents.

Along with studies, we focus on sports, activities, and overall development so that students become responsible, disciplined, and capable individuals. Our goal is to prepare students to face future challenges and become proud citizens of India. Along with studies, we focus on sports and activities to support balanced growth.

Facilities for students

Hostel Facilities

Our school provides safe and well-maintained hostel facilities for both boys and girls. Each hostel is supervised by trained wardens to ensure discipline, comfort, and proper care. Students receive healthy meals, study support, and a homely environment that helps them focus on learning and personal growth

Playground

We have a large playground that allows students to take part in various sports and outdoor activities. It provides a perfect space for physical fitness, teamwork, and recreation. Regular sports events and games are organized to help students stay active, energetic, and healthy throughout the year.

Science Exhibitions

The school organizes regular science exhibitions to encourage creativity and practical learning among students. These events allow children to present models, experiments, and innovative ideas. By participating in exhibitions, students build confidence, improve scientific thinking, and develop a deeper understanding of the subjects they study.

Classes from Pre-Nursery to XII

We offer education from Pre-Nursery to Class 12, covering students aged 3 to 18. This allows children to complete their entire schooling journey in one place. Our curriculum supports each age group with the right guidance, helping students grow step-by-step towards academic and personal success.

About Us

Our school was established in 1997 by Mr. Naba Kumar Roy, the father of our current Secretary, Mr. Karun Kumar Roy. With over 25 years of experience in providing quality education, our institution has grown from a single branch in Dumka, Jharkhand, to 8 branches across Dumka, Sejokhar, Jama, Ramgarh, Deoghar, and Jamtara.

Our primary aim is to offer top-class education to all students. We have a team of 50+ well-experienced teachers across all branches who are dedicated to nurturing young minds. Along with academics, we also prioritize the health and overall development of our students by organizing regular sports events and activities.
